Higher alcohols, often referred to as fusel alcohols, are alcohols with more than two carbon atoms. These include various types such as butanol, pentanol, hexanol, and higher homologues. They are used in a multitude of applications, including solvents, plasticizers, detergents, pharmaceuticals, and as intermediates in chemical synthesis.
